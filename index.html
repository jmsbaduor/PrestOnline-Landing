e-Pharmaceutical Product Supply System

Prest Online is a web application that gives pharmaceutical retailers access to numerous suppliers in real time.

What our solution offers

Enabling retailers get up-to-date pricing of requested products in real time.

Control.

Prest online gives pharmaceutical retailers control to decide the time for responses.

Best Prices.

Product price responses are automatically filtered to display the best prices so retailers can make a value for money decision.

For Retailers

1. Access to numerous suppliers at your disposal

2. Get real time prices of items from multiple suppliers

3. Get best prices during product purchases

4. Receive real time info from suppliers about promotional sales and re-stocked items

5. Make in App purchase requests to suppliers
